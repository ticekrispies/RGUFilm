Yearly events:

"Here at the film club we hold events all year round, including monthly trips to the cinema to see the up-and-coming movies. We also travel a little further away such as the annual day trip to London to see the prestigious Sir Alfred Hitcock's home and a visit to the London Film Museum in covent Garden. We even manage to fit in some shopping. Of course we have some trips closer to home, visiting the Rosslyn Chapel in Midlothian, famous for its use in the 2006 thriller, The Da Vinci Code. Other events include pub quizzes, creating our own short films, acting and directing workshops and even the occasional bun sale. We hold fundraising to pay for buses for trips and for the use of the equipment so please participate when you can, as all help is greatly appreciated!"
